## Open-source license: AGPLv3

The open-source version of NISB is licensed under the GNU Affero General Public License v3.0.

Under AGPLv3, you may use, study, modify, and redistribute NISB as long as you comply with AGPLv3.

AGPLv3 can be used in commercial settings too, provided the AGPLv3 obligations are met.

In particular, if you modify NISB and make the modified version available to users over a network, AGPLv3 may require you to provide the corresponding source code of your modified version to those users.

## Commercial license

A commercial license is available for users or organizations that need terms different from AGPLv3.

A commercial license may be appropriate if you want to:

- Build a proprietary product based on NISB
- Build a closed-source integration based on NISB
- Embed NISB into a commercial product under non-AGPL terms
- Offer a hosted or SaaS service based on modified NISB code without using AGPLv3 terms
- Privately redistribute modified versions under custom terms
- Negotiate custom enterprise terms for partnership, investment, integration, deployment, or redistribution

A commercial license is not required merely because you use NISB at work or in a business setting, as long as your use complies with AGPLv3.

If you are unsure whether your use case complies with AGPLv3, consult legal counsel.
